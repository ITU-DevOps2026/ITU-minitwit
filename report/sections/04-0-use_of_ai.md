In this project we have used AI for different purposes. These purposes being:
* Debugging, especially for determining where a bug/bottleneck is located in our system.
* Discussing solutions/packages to use in our project.
* Explaining concepts and file formats the group did not have experience with.
* Suggesting best practices for structuring a distributed system.
* Helping translate or interpret error messages and logs that were unclear or unfamiliar.

Different AI models were used across the group. Here they are listed, in no particular order:
* [ChatGPT](https://chatgpt.com/)
* [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/app?hl=da)
* [Claude](https://claude.ai/)

### How did AI support or hinder our learning
It has supported us in explaining concepts and helping the group members get better at locating bugs and more in a distributed system. It especially also helped us in discussing the upsides and downsides of using different packages for a feature we needed to implement.

It has hindered us by sometimes providing overly confident answers that turned out to be incorrect, leading us to spend extra time verifying or debugging solutions we initially trusted. In continuation of this, it sometimes proposed solutions that destroyed other parts of the program that functioned before.
